About me

Bio

I was born in San Diego. When I was very young, my family moved to the Bay Area. We bounced around for a little bit near Berkeley before settling in the small town of Martinez.

Martinez is actually the hometown of naturalist John Muir!

Sometime in high school, I became interested in computer science, and in college, I was enamored with web development. I decided to pursue this as my career path!

Skills and Education

I'm currently a third-year Math-Computer Science major at UCSD. My skills currently encompass HTML, CSS, and Javascript, and also React, Tailwind, and Next.js (sorry Prof. Powell). Also, if you ever need a qualified ice cream/dessert expert, I've worked at both Baskin-Robbins and Matcha Cafe Maiko.
